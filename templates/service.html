About Us

Rooted in Reforestation, Growing Sustainability

Who We Are

Green Future Society is a national non-governmental organization formed in October 2015 and registered in March 2016 under the Registration of Societies Act of 2005. Headquartered in Kisumu, Kenya, our reach extends across the Western Kenya region.

We focus on promoting tree planting, protection, and sustainable agricultural practices to mitigate the effects of climate change and foster food security for present and future generations.

Our Objectives

Environmental Conservation

We aim to increase forest cover in Kenya by promoting afforestation, reforestation, and the protection of natural habitats, especially among the younger generation to build a long-term conservation culture.

Sustainable Agriculture

We promote modern farming technologies while safeguarding native crops to enhance food security and adapt agriculture to climate change.

Who We Are

Green Future Society is a national non-governmental organization formed in October 2015 and registered in March 2016 under the Registration of Societies Act of 2005. Headquartered in Kisumu, Kenya, our reach extends across the Western Kenya region.

We focus on promoting tree planting, protection, and sustainable agricultural practices to mitigate the effects of climate change and foster food security for present and future generations.

Our Vision

Offering strategic economic base for sustainable livelihood.

Our Mission

Increasing tree cover to mitigate climate change and foster community development by using waste lands, hill tops and on-farm agro-forestry through community awareness and education. We support government to achieve at least 10% forest cover by 2030.

Our Goal

To contribute towards alleviating climate change and ensuring economic stability.

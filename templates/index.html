Our Mission

Our Green Legacy

At the heart of our initiative is the commitment to empower farmers, restore forests, and promote sustainable agriculture in Kenya. Through seedling supply, nursery support, and community-based reforestation, we're building a greener, more resilient future.

Our Background

Green Future Society is a National non-governmental organization formed in October 2015 and registered in March 2016 under the Registration of Society's Act of 2005. Our headquarters is in Kisumu, Kenya, covering Western Kenya region.

Learn more

Our Goal

Supporting the Kenyan government in increasing forest cover to mitigate the effects of climate change and enhance agricultural activities to improve food security across the nation.
